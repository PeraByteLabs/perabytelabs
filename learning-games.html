CORE™ Skills Developed

Our learning games are specifically designed to develop key skills from the Cognitive and Operational pillars of the CORE™ Framework.

🧠

Critical Thinking

Games that challenge players to analyze information objectively, evaluate evidence, and make reasoned judgments through puzzle-solving and strategic decision-making.

🔍

Problem Solving

Interactive challenges that teach systematic approaches to identifying problems, generating solutions, and implementing strategies through trial and improvement.

✨

Creative Thinking

Open-ended gameplay that encourages original ideas, unconventional solutions, and thinking beyond traditional boundaries through imaginative scenarios.

🎯

Planning & Goal Setting

Strategy games that develop the ability to envision desired outcomes, create pathways to achieve them, and organize resources effectively.

🔬

Concentration & Memory

Focused gameplay that strengthens sustained attention while developing effective strategies for storing and retrieving information.

🌟

Curiosity

Exploration-based games that foster intrinsic motivation to discover, investigate, and understand through active questioning and discovery.
